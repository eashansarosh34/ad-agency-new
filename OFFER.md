# The Offer — One Product, One Price, No Custom Quotes

---

## The Single Line

> **₹14,999/month. Real leads, live dashboard, cancel anytime. First diagnosis free.**

That is the entire offer. You never say "marketing services." You never quote a custom price. You never send a proposal document. This is it.

---

## What They Get (Fixed Scope)

| What | Detail |
|---|---|
| Meta Ads (Facebook + Instagram) | Campaigns set up, monitored, and auto-optimised daily by the system |
| Real lead tracking | Every enquiry logged in a live dashboard — name, time, source |
| Weekly WhatsApp summary | Plain-English numbers every Monday: leads, cost-per-lead, what changed |
| Monthly performance report | Full PDF with trends, wins, and the plan for next month |
| AI content suggestions | Trending ad angles for their niche, refreshed automatically |
| Direct contact | You, reachable on WhatsApp — not a support ticket |

**Ad spend is separate** — they pay Meta directly. Your fee is ₹14,999 flat.

---

## What Is NOT Included (Protect Your Time)

- Website redesign or development
- Logo / branding / photography
- Google Ads (offer as a separate upsell at month 3+)
- SEO content writing
- Custom reports or custom dashboards
- "Can you also just quickly..." anything not in the table above

If they ask for something not in scope: *"That's outside our standard package — let me check what that would look like as an add-on."* Then decide if it's worth it.

## Objection Kills

**"It's too expensive."**
> "Totally fair. What are you paying per patient right now to acquire them through word of mouth or Instagram alone? If we bring you 5 real enquiries a month and you close 2, does ₹14,999 pay for itself?" (Shut up and let them do the maths.)

**"We tried ads before and they didn't work."**
> "That's actually the most common thing we hear — and it's almost always the same reason: the campaign ran but nobody was monitoring it daily. This system checks performance every few hours and adjusts automatically. It's a different thing."

**"I need to think about it."**
> "Of course — no rush at all. The report is yours either way. I'll check back in next week."
(Follow up in 7 days, once. After that, move on.)

**"Can you do it cheaper?"**
> "We don't do custom pricing — it keeps things simple for both sides. But if budget is the constraint, we can start with a smaller ad spend and scale up once you're seeing leads come in."

**"Can I see results first?"**
> "The Trojan Report you already have is the preview — it's the same diagnostic we run on live clients. The live dashboard means you'd see every lead in real time from day one, not at the end of the month."

## Why Fixed Price Works

- **No proposals** = no wasted time on people who were never going to pay
- **No negotiation** = you attract clients who value the outcome, not clients who want the cheapest option
- **No scope creep** = the table above is the product; everything else is a conversation
- **Easy referrals** = clients can explain your offer to someone else in one sentence
- **You know your economics** = ₹14,999 × 10 clients = ₹1,49,990/month. The system runs most of it.
