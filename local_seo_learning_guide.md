# Local SEO / Google Business Profile — What to Actually Learn

This channel is different from the others on the roadmap: there's no API to wire up and
walk away from. The agent above drafts text for you, but the strategy underneath it is a
real skill. Here's the actual substance, in priority order.

---

## 1. Profile completeness (do this first, for every client)
- Every category filled in correctly — primary category matters most for ranking, pick
  the most specific one that fits (e.g. "Dental Clinic" beats generic "Medical Clinic")
- Hours always accurate, including holiday hours — wrong hours actively hurts trust
- Services/products list filled in completely, using the exact words customers search
  (not internal jargon)
- At least 10+ real, recent photos — interior, exterior, team, work examples. Profiles
  with photos get meaningfully more clicks than ones without
- Business description uses natural local-intent language once, not stuffed with keywords

## 2. Reviews — the single highest-leverage lever
- Volume and recency matter more than perfection — a steady trickle of new reviews beats
  a pile of old ones
- **The actual unlock: ask at the right moment.** The best time is right after a good
  experience, not days later. This is where it connects to what's already built — once
  Agent 04 marks a lead "qualified" and the appointment happens, that's the natural moment
  for a WhatsApp follow-up asking for a review. Worth wiring that in once this channel is
  live.
- Respond to every review, good or bad, within a few days — use the agent above for the
  draft, but always read it before posting
- Never offer anything in exchange for a review (gift cards, discounts) — this violates
  Google's policies and risks the listing

## 3. Posts — consistency over cleverness
- Aim for 1–2 posts a week, not daily — stale profiles look abandoned, but spammy posting
  doesn't help either
- Posts with a clear offer or update outperform vague "we're great!" posts
- Use the agent above to draft these in batches (e.g. a month at a time) so this never
  becomes a weekly scramble

## 4. Citation consistency (NAP — Name, Address, Phone)
- The exact same business name, address, and phone number need to appear identically
  across every directory the business is listed on (Justdial, Sulekha, Facebook, the
  clinic's own website footer, etc.)
- Inconsistencies (e.g. "Dr. Mehta Dental Clinic" vs "Mehta Dental Clinic" vs missing
  suite number) confuse Google's confidence in the listing and can quietly hurt ranking
- This is manual audit work the first time, then just needs occasional rechecking

## 5. What NOT to do
- Never use a fake/virtual address for a Service Area Business if there's no real walk-in
  location — Google actively suspends listings for this
- Don't buy reviews or use review-gating (only asking happy customers, hiding the request
  from unhappy ones) — both are against Google's policies and carry real suspension risk

---

## What's still missing for full automation later
Pulling reviews and posting automatically (instead of copy-pasting agent output by hand)
needs the **Google Business Profile API**, which requires a Google Cloud project and has
historically had a more restrictive approval process than Meta's APIs — budget more lead
time for this than the Meta integrations took. Worth applying for once you're managing
more than 2-3 client profiles and the manual copy-paste starts costing real time.
